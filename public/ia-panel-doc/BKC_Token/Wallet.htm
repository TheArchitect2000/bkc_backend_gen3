BKC Wallet

BKC Wallet is blockchain-based wallet to keep tracks of your tokens. You can monitor, transfer, import and extract tokens by BKC Wallet. 

 

Monitor

You can track information of your token on BKC Wallet as follow:

  • Current Balance shows the number of tokens in your wallet.
  • Token Graph shows the trend of your balance.
  • History shows the details of every transaction in your wallet. 

 

Transfer

You can transfer your tokens to other Wallet ID as follow:

  1. Click on Transfer button.
  2. Enter the number of tokens you want to transfer.
  3. Enter Wallet ID of the reciever.
  4. Click on the Ok button.

 

Import

You can import tokens into your wallet by a voucher as follow:

  1. Click on Import button
  2. Enter the voucher
  3. Click on the Ok button.

 

Extract

You can extract your tokens into a voucher as follow:

  1. Click on Extract button.
  2. Enter the number of tokens you want to extract.
  3. Click on the Ok button.

   Note: Your extracted vouchers is listed in the Voucher List.
