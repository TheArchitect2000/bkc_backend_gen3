BKC Token Card

BKC Token Card provides a shortcut to your BKC Wallet. BKC Wallets keeps tracks of your BKC tokens. BKC tokens is blockchain-based token that enables the device owner to sell/buy BKC Services. You can send or receive BKC tokens from your wallet to other wallets. BKC Toke Card shows the summary of BKC Wallet as follows:

  • Balance shows the number of token in your wallet.
  • Open Wallet gives access to your wallet.
  • History shows the last transactions of your wallet.
