Navigating on BKC Control Panel

To navigate on different parts of BKC Control Panel you can use Left Menu and Header Bar.

 

Left Menu

On the Left Menu you can see the name of your home. Also you can access to the main parts of the BKC Control Panel:

  • Home gives information of daytime and weather as well as the trend of activities in last 30 days.
  • Device provides access to each device installed in your system.
  • Service provids access to each service installed in your system.
  • Service Market provide unlimited services that can be installed in your system.
  • Service Creator provides access to two consoles for creating new services.
  • Setting provide various adjustment of your system.
  • Help & Support gives you instruction to use BKC Control Panel.

 

 

Header Bar

On the Header Bar you can access to three functionality:

  • Notifications warn you about last notifications.
  • Guard lets you to adjust your home into guard.
  • BKC Tokens provides access to BKC Wallet.
